My name is Emily McCullough, but my friends call me Em.
I’m a twenty year old Professional Writing student with a double major in Criminal Justice at Michigan State University.
One day, I hope to have my dream job working for the Federal Bureau of Investigations (FBI).
I’m currently a member of the Michigan State University Women’s Club Water Polo team,
where I hold the position of Apparel Chair. This means that I design the logos and logo wear for the team.
I passionately enjoy travelling, with my favorite trip so far being to Yellowstone National Park.
I have plans in the future to travel to Europe as well as Banff National Park.
I enjoy large cups of tea, long books, and my cats.
